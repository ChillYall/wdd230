Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide focused advocacy for business growth and sustainable development through the provision of business services for the advancement of grassroots entrepreneurship, chamber development, business innovation and excellence, and operational efficiency. These will be accomplished through the efforts of a professional organization working in close collaboration with various stakeholders from the public and private sectors within the city.
